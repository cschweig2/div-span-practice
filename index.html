Cedar Trees

10/14/20

Today I'll be writing about cedar trees. Cedar trees are evergreen trees that typically have a flat scaling leaf pattern. Many cedars are prized for their aromatic woods. That's all I know about cedar trees. I'm a tree, not a professor.
